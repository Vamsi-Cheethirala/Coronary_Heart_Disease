üí° What is coronary heart disease (CHD)?

CHD is a condition where the blood vessels that supply the heart muscle become narrowed or blocked. This can lead to chest pain, shortness of breath, or heart attacks.

üìä What are the 13 input features?

The inputs include medical and lifestyle indicators such as:

  • Age, Sex, Chest Pain Type
  • Resting Blood Pressure, Cholesterol
  • Fasting Blood Sugar, Rest ECG Results
  • Max Heart Rate Achieved, Exercise-induced Angina
  • Oldpeak (ST depression), ST slope, Thalassemia

ü§ñ How does this tool work?

We use machine learning models trained on real patient data to analyze your input and estimate your risk of CHD. Four models are used: Random Forest, Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, and KNN.

No. This tool is for educational purposes only and not a replacement for a professional medical consultation. Always consult your doctor for medical decisions.

Accuracy depends on the model and input data. On our training dataset, Random Forest performed best with around 85% accuracy.
